3.2. Device Permissions accessed by apps.

We request certain permission on our apps to access certain features on your devices to provide a good user experience and functionality on our apps.

On ANDROID Devices:

On Android 6.0 and above: The app will request these permissions when required and you may choose to accept or deny the permission (please note that some permissions are mandatory for the proper functioning of the app).

On Android 5.1.1 or below: It will ask you to grant all permissions for your app at install-time. If you click Accept at install-time, all permissions the app requests are granted. If you deny the permissions request, the system will cancel the installation of the app.

Important permissions requested are:

Storage

Name: android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Usage: This permission gives us access to read from and write to local storage on your device.
Why do we need it? Since our apps use music content, this music content is downloaded to your device and stored on your local storage and read from your storage for offline playback. We do not read any other content from your local storage other than the files the app creates or downloads.
Is it mandatory? Yes. This permission is mandatory since the app cannot function as intended without being able to download and read the music files.

Microphone (Optional)

Name: permission android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO
Usage: This permission gives us access to the microphone on your device.
Why do we need it? The microphone permissions are used to provide music visualization on music playback. This enables the app to retrieve part of the currently playing audio for visualization purpose. It is not an audio recording interface.
Is it mandatory? NO. This is an optional permission. On Android 6.0 and above, you can choose to deny this permission. On Android 5.1.1 or below, this permission is granted on app install, but you can choose to turn off the visualizer in AUDIO SETTINGS under SETTINGS in your app. The app will no longer use the microphone permission.
